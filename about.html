About ChnNews

Cambodia's trusted English-language digital media platform since 2020

Who We Are

ChnCam (news.chncam.site) is Cambodia's premier English-language digital news and culture platform, dedicated to bringing authentic, balanced, and insightful coverage of everything happening across the Kingdom of Wonder. Founded in Phnom Penh in 2020, we have grown into one of the most trusted sources of English-language information about Cambodia for both local residents and international readers.

Our team of experienced journalists, cultural writers, and local experts are passionate about telling Cambodia's story — from the corridors of government in Phnom Penh to the ancient temple complexes of Siem Reap, from the pristine beaches of Sihanoukville to the vibrant markets of Battambang. We believe that Cambodia deserves world-class journalism that reflects the depth, complexity, and richness of this extraordinary country.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to inform, inspire, and connect people through quality journalism about Cambodia. We strive to provide our readers with accurate, fair, and timely reporting across news, travel, culture, business, lifestyle, and technology — helping them understand and appreciate the Kingdom of Wonder in all its dimensions.

We are committed to journalistic integrity, editorial independence, and the highest standards of responsible reporting. We do not accept payment for editorial coverage. Our advertising is clearly labeled and kept entirely separate from our journalism.

What We Cover

  • National & Regional News — Politics, governance, social issues, and international relations affecting Cambodia and Southeast Asia
  • Business & Economy — Investment, trade, startups, markets, and Cambodia's rapidly developing economy
  • Travel & Tourism — Destination guides, travel tips, visa information, and the latest on Cambodia's tourism industry
  • Culture & Heritage — Khmer arts, traditions, festivals, UNESCO sites, and the preservation of Cambodia's living culture
  • Lifestyle — Food, restaurants, wellness, expat life, and day-to-day living in Cambodia
  • Technology — Digital transformation, fintech, startups, and Cambodia's growing tech ecosystem
  • Environment — Conservation, the Mekong River, climate, and environmental policy in Cambodia

Our Values

Accuracy

We verify all information from multiple sources before publishing. Corrections are issued promptly and transparently when errors occur.

Independence

Our editorial decisions are made independently. Advertisers have no influence over our coverage or content.

Fairness

We present all perspectives fairly and give subjects of our reporting the opportunity to respond before publication.

Community

We are committed to serving both Cambodia's local communities and the international audience that cares about this remarkable country.
